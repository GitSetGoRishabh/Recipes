Garlic Naan


This Garlic Naan bread is light, soft, fresh, and filled with flavor! It is so good that you will be making it again and again. I show you how to make Garlic Naan on a skillet on a stovetop. In my recipe the dough is infused with fresh garlic making it extra delicious. Serve the Restaurant Style Garlic Naan Bread as a side for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.


Ingredients

for naan dough

  • All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • Warm water & milk
  • Active dry yeast or instant yeast
  • Sugar
  • Plain yogurt or Greek yogurt
  • Oil (vegetable or neutral)
  • Salt
  • Baking powder (optional)

for garlic topping

  • melted butter
  • minced garlic
  • fresh cilantro chopped
  • nigella seeds

Steps

  1. Activate Yeast: Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast; let sit for 5-10 mins until foamy
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients: Combine flour, salt, and baking powder (if using) in a large bowl.
  3. Combine Wet & Dry: Add the yeast mixture, yogurt, oil, and some minced garlic to the flour. Mix to form a shaggy dough.
  4. Knead Dough: Turn onto a floured surface and knead for 3-5 minutes until smooth and soft, adding a little extra flour if too sticky.
  5. First Rise: Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  6. Shape Naan: Divide dough into balls, then roll each into an oval or teardrop shape (about 1/8-inch thick).
  7. Add Topping: Sprinkle minced garlic, cilantro, and nigella seeds (if using) on one side and lightly roll to adhere.
  8. Cook Naan: Cook on a hot skillet (tawa) or griddle over medium-high heat for a minute per side, until puffed and spotted. Brush with melted butter immediately after cooking.
