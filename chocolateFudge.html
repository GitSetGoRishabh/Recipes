Chocolate fudge


Chocolate fudge typically uses chocolate (chips or bars), sweetened condensed milk or evaporated milk, butter, and vanilla, often with additions like nuts, with recipes ranging from simple 3-ingredient versions to more traditional stovetop methods requiring sugar and careful temperature control for that classic creamy, melt-in-your-mouth texture.


Ingredients

  • 1 (14 oz) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 12-16 oz Chocolate Chips (semi-sweet, milk, or dark) or chopped chocolate
  • 1-2 tbsp Butter (optional, for richness)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract (optional)
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans) (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Pan: Line an 8x8 inch pan with parchment paper or foil, leaving an overhang.
  2. Melt: In a saucepan over low heat (or microwave), combine condensed milk, butter, and chocolate chips.
  3. Stir: Stir continuously until smooth and melted.
  4. Add Flavor: Remove from heat and stir in vanilla and nuts if using.
  5. Pour & Chill: Pour into the prepared pan, spread evenly, and refrigerate for 1-2 hours until firm.
  6. Cut: Lift by the parchment and cut into squares.
