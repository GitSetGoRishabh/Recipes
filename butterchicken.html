Butter Chicken


Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) is a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced Indian curry featuring marinated chicken pieces simmered in a velvety tomato, butter, and cream sauce, characterized by its vibrant color and balance of sweet, tangy, and savory flavors from ingredients like ginger, garlic, garam masala, and the signature kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves). A typical recipe involves marinating chicken in yogurt and spices, cooking it (often grilled or pan-fried), then simmering it in a base of butter, tomatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, and cream with whole spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon for depth, finishing with fresh cream and kasuri methi.


Ingredients

for marinade

  • Chicken (boneless, cubed)
  • Yogurt (plain)
  • Ginger-garlic paste or minced
  • Spices: Turmeric, red chili powder (Kashmiri for color), garam masala, cumin
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt

for gravy

  • butter/ghee
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • ginger-garlic
  • whole spices
  • ground spices
  • kasuri methi
  • chashews
  • heavy cream
  • sugar or honey
  • salt
  • fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic, and dry spices; let it marinate for at least 30 mins (or longer in the fridge)
  2. Cook Chicken: Pan-fry, grill, or bake the marinated chicken pieces until cooked through and lightly charred.
  3. Make Gravy Base: Saut√© whole spices, onion, ginger, and garlic in butter/oil until fragrant. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, and ground spices; cook until the oil separates.
  4. Blend (Optional): For a smoother gravy, blend the tomato mixture before adding cream.
  5. Combine & Simmer: Add cooked chicken to the gravy. Stir in cream, sugar/honey, and kasuri methi; simmer gently for a few minutes, being careful not to boil vigorously after adding cream.
  6. Garnish & Serve: Finish with more butter and fresh cream, garnish with cilantro, and serve hot with naan or rice.
